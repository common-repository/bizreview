Google Business Review Showcase Plugin - Easily Show Your Google Business Reviews On Your Website

== Description ==
Bizreview Business and Google Place Review WordPress Plugin for display Google Reviews & Ratings to increase user confidence, SEO and traffic of your Business. This plugin help you to increase sales with social proof. This plguin has 14 different option to showcase your Google place reviews. There has option to generate shortcodes so you will able to use this shortcode in your pages, posts or anywhere you want in your WordPress website. The shortcode also can be use in any popular page builders like Gutenburg, Elementor or WpBekary. If you use elementor you have elementor widgets to use.

1. Upload plugin directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'BizReview' menu in WordPress.
